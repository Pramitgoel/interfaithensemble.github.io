Cultural Heritage Essay Competition 2024

Share your insights and experiences about the rich cultural heritage of your state through one of our themed questions.

Distinguished Evaluation Panel

All essays are evaluated by our esteemed panel of graduates from:

Stanford University Harvard University Indian Institutes of Management (IIM)

Essay Questions

  1. "Describe a unique cultural tradition or practice from your state. How has it evolved over time?" What is its significance to you personally as well as to your culture and state?
  2. "Describe the unique cuisine from your state. How has it evolved over time?" What is its significance to you personally as well as to your culture and state?
  3. "Describe the unique style of music from your state. How has it evolved over time?" What is its significance to you personally as well as to your culture and state?
  4. "Describe the unique style of art from your state. How has it evolved over time?" What is its significance to you personally as well as to your culture and state?

Guidelines

  • Word limit: 800 words per essay
  • Choose ONE question from the four options above
  • Each question category will have separate awards
  • Essays must be original and previously unpublished
  • Include specific examples and personal experiences in your essay
  • Proper citations required for any referenced sources
  • Essays should be written in English
  • All winning essays will be published on our website
  • Deadline for submission: February 15, 2024
  • Essays will be graded on a rolling bases as they are submitted
